Weekly Summary

Multiple cybersecurity incidents have affected major companies and organizations, including a breach at Dell, a cyber attack targeting journalism professionals in Britain, and the TfL cyber attack affecting passenger data access. The TfL attack has resulted in restrictions on customer data access and is still ongoing. In other news, China has urged vigilance against Taiwanese cyber attacks, and consumer technology news reports on Russian cyber-influence operations and Apple raising battery replacement prices. Singaporean finance companies should be aware of these incidents and take appropriate measures to protect themselves from potential attacks.
